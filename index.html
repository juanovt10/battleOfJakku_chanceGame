The battle of Jakku

While Rebel troops engage the imperial fleet on Tattooine, your mission is to retrieve a vital map from the planet of Jakku.

Unfortunately, the imperial forces have identified this planet as a rebel stronghold and deployed a solitary ship to guard it against any rebel activity. It's now up to you to vanquish the imperial ship, secure the map from Jakku, and ultimately save the galaxy!

How to play:

  • Cover to prevent taking any damage from the enemy
  • Reload to replenish your ammo count, but be aware that you will be defenseless against enemy attacks during this time
  • Shoot to damge the imperial ship shields
