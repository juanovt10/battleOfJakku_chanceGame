The battle of Jaku!

As other Rebel troops are distracting the imperial fleet in the planet of Tattoine, you have been assigned to go and collect an important map in the Jaku planet

Unfortunatly, imperial forces recognise this planet as rebel interest and left one single ship guarding the planet in case of rebel contact. Is up to you to defeat the imperial ship and collect the map from Jaku to save the Galaxy!

How to play:

  • Evade the enemy to avoid recieving any damage
  • Reload to increase you ammo count on the left, but here you will be defensless in case of enemy attact
  • Shoot to damge the imperial ship shields
